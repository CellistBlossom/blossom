Soundfonts

This page is dedicated to soundfonts I've created! Right now there's not a lot here, but there might be more in the future. Click the links below to download them!

Chicken Soundfont - Mostly made as a joke, not really intended to sound great. Includes two presets with slightly different chicken sound samples and not much else.

Grub Version 1.3 - Grubby! Uses samples of grub sounds from Hollow Knight. Includes two samples and the following presets:

🐛 Grub: Low notes use Grubfather sound, high notes use grub sound

🐛 Grub Chorus: Same as above but it's played at the same time as a slightly detuned version of the same thing (so it sounds kinda like multiple grubbies singing)

🐛 Little Grub: Only the grub sound

🐛 Grubking: Only the Grubfather sound
