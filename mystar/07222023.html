fragility

07/22/2023

i spent today sick.

it's been a long time since ive had this specific sort of sickness, the one not caused by any virus. i hope that it's a one-off thing and not a sign of worse things to come. but because it came on suddenly and i dont think i did anything to cause it im definitely worried

it reminded me that life is so fragile. it really could be over in an instant at any time. if you could be taken away so suddenly, without a good explanation, then who's to say that wont happen to me? to anyone i know? the universe is so large and we are so small

we take the current moment for granted, or at least i do. i feel like i need to learn to appreciate it more. understand that none of this is permanent. not even the stars in the sky.

when will i see you again?

i love you

always yours,

blossom
